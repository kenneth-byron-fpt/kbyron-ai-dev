- Emerging as best practice

## Communication Principles

### Make It Scannable

- **Headlines first**: Key takeaway in 10 words
- **Bullet points**: Not paragraphs
- **Bold key terms**: Aid skimming
- **Visual hierarchy**: Use emojis, headers, formatting
- **Time estimates**: "5 min read" so executives can plan

### Make It Actionable

Every briefing should have:
- ✅ **"So What?"**: Why this matters to you
- ✅ **"Now What?"**: What action to take
- ✅ **"Who/When?"**: Who owns next steps, by when

### Make It Strategic

Focus on:
- Big picture trends (not daily noise)
- Competitive implications (not just technology)
- Business opportunities (not just technical details)
- Leadership decisions (not tactical implementation)

### Make It Trustworthy

- **Cite sources**: Link to original content
- **Show confidence level**: "High confidence" vs. "Emerging signal"
- **Admit unknowns**: "We need more data on X"
- **Update when wrong**: "Previous analysis was incorrect because..."
